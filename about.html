About Us

Company History

Established in 2023, Marvelous Corporation - A trademark exclusively registered in France is proudly a manufacturer and supplier of fashion products integrating exclusive advanced technology. Marvelous Corporation has constantly been making creative efforts to develop products to serve the fashion needs of the future market, with modern equipment, technology, and highly qualified and creative human resources.

In 2023, Marvelous launched MERO-the first self-designed product brand, and generated $2.3 billion in profit after tax with revenue of $ 22.4 billion. As of mid-2020, not only MERO, Marvelous continues to develop CASE-the series of accessories for MERO used for charging and cleaning with sound waves and UV radiation.

In June 2050, with breakthrough features that cannot be found in any other products, MERO attracted and satisfied a significant number of passengers.
